Who is Co{Duels}?

Co{Duels} was created by Michael Bae, Ivan Loughman-Pawelko, Linda Kung, and Seema Ullal as their final project at Fullstack Academy. Things we : Laughing, snacks, coffee, arena.js, puppies, modals, and cookies.

We made Co{Duels} because we wanted a fun, social, and easy to use way to practice programming.
